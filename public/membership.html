Membership

Want to use the workshop whenever?

Our mission is to make this space available to everyone who wants to use it, regardless of financial means. Our facilities are open to the general public whenever a member is around to have unlocked the building, and during a number of scheduled events and open-house nights. We offer keys, 24/7 access, and organizational voting rights to community members who contribute to the space in some way, be it through dues, donations, or their time and skills.

We Want You Here!

Money helps. You can help more.

If you need space to work on personal projects, we want to do whatever we can to help you out. Even if you don't have any money.

We have a large variety of pricing options, but we're also very flexible. If you can donate time or resources, that goes a long way with us, and will cover a premium membership for you. Show up at our weekly Wednesday meeting, and give us your ideas. Or post on the forums. This isn't theoretical; we already have a few members that do this.

Of course, we still need money to pay taxes and keep the lights on. So if you can help with fund-raising, that's hugely appreciated.
